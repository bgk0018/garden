## Highlights

>[!QUOTE]  
>Stories Resonate, Rules Fade
>Summary:
>Human memory thrives on narratives rather than mere statistics or rules.
>Institutions aiming to promote ethical behavior find greater success not in presenting rules, but in sharing compelling stories. Research highlights that storytelling engages audiences far better than simply citing regulations.
>An example from the U.S. Department of Defense illustrates this: instead of relaying dry rules, a lawyer created a collection of real-life ethical failures that caught attention and conveyed messages more effectively.
>This approach underlines the power of storytelling in influencing behavior and ensuring messages stick.
