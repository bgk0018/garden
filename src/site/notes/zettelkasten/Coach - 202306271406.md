# Content

Supports a [[zettelkasten/Creator - 202306271318\|Creator]] by asking questions that help clarify the [[zettelkasten/Creator - 202306271318\|Creator]]'s outcomes or helps them to see reality more clearly.[^1]

Unlike a [[zettelkasten/Rescuer - 202305271811\|Rescuer]], a [[zettelkasten/Coach - 202306271406\|Coach]] will never take away a [[zettelkasten/Creator - 202306271318\|Creator]]'s power to choose their own responses and actions.[^2]

There are three things a [[zettelkasten/Coach - 202306271406\|Coach]] helps get clarity on:[^3]

- Clarify the [[zettelkasten/Outcome - 202304261242\|Outcome]] you want to create
- Speak the truth about your current reality ([[zettelkasten/Dynamic Tension - 202304171455\|Dynamic Tension]])
- Get clear about the [[zettelkasten/Baby Step - 202304261244\|Baby Step]]s you're going to take.
