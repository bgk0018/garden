# Content

The solution the [[zettelkasten/Victim - 202305251726\|Victim]] finds for their [[zettelkasten/Persecutor - 202305271800\|Persecutor]].

A [[zettelkasten/Rescuer - 202305271811\|Rescuer]] doesn't need to be a person, just something to ease the [[zettelkasten/Fear - 202409220942\|Fear]] felt by the [[zettelkasten/Victim - 202305251726\|Victim]] caused by the [[zettelkasten/Persecutor - 202305271800\|Persecutor]].

[[zettelkasten/Rescuer - 202305271811\|Rescuers]] show up in a few ways:

- Sought out by the [[zettelkasten/Victim - 202305251726\|Victim]]
- The [[zettelkasten/Rescuer - 202305271811\|Rescuer]] intervenes
- An imagined outcome by the [[zettelkasten/Victim - 202305251726\|Victim]] that they hope will show up to rescue them  
[^1]

This role is defined through the prism of the [[zettelkasten/Victim - 202305251726\|Victim]].

The [[zettelkasten/Rescuer - 202305271811\|Rescuer]] takes action based on [[zettelkasten/Fear - 202409220942\|Fear]] as well. They fear they will not be needed and seek action for a sense of purpose. [^2]
