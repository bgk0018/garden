# Content

An [[zettelkasten/Internal Shift - 202306291522\|Internal Shift]] occurs when you change the way you think and respond to what's going on around you. By first [[zettelkasten/Mindset Shifting - 202306291518\|Mindset Shifting]] to see yourself as a [[zettelkasten/Creator - 202306271318\|Creator]], you're able to see others as [[zettelkasten/Creator - 202306271318\|Creator]]s as well and not just [[zettelkasten/Problem - 202304261228\|Problems]].[^1]

An [[zettelkasten/Internal Shift - 202306291522\|Internal Shift]] creates an [[zettelkasten/External Shift - 202306291523\|External Shift]].
