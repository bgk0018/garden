A significant political strategy employed by [[Donald Trump\|Donald Trump]] and allies to question election legitimacy, particularly following the [[2020 Election\|2020 Election]] and leading into [[2024 Election\|2024 Election]].

## Key Components
- Pre-emptive claims of potential fraud
- Focus on [[Mail In Voting\|Mail In Voting]] concerns
- Allegations about [[archive/2024/projects/Literature Notes/Non Citizen Voting\|Non Citizen Voting]]
- Challenges to overseas ballot legitimacy

## Implementation Strategy
The approach includes:
- Legal infrastructure (130+ lawsuits filed)
- 230,000+ trained poll watchers
- [[RNC\|RNC]] coordination
- Social media messaging

## Historical Context
Builds on [[2020 Election\|2020 Election]] narrative:
- [[January 6 Capitol Attack\|January 6 Capitol Attack]] aftermath
- Multiple court challenges
- State-level election law changes
- [[Stop the Steal\|Stop the Steal]] movement
