>[!summary]- Description
> During his first six weeks in office, President Donald Trump has worked to tear down the federal government and attack long-standing institutions. He's undertaken a purge at the FBI and Department of Justice. He's forced out federal officials who are responsible for overseeing ethics and protecting whistleblowers.
