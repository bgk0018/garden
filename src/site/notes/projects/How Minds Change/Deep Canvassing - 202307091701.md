# Content

A method of persuasion used by [[projects/How Minds Change/The Leadership LAB - 202307091706\|The Leadership LAB]].[^1]

This uses [[projects/How Minds Change/Conversations instead of debates - 202307091648\|conversations instead of debates]] and [[projects/How Minds Change/Focus on the lived experience to change a mind - 202307091716\|focuses on the lived experience to change a mind]] in a face to face format. Interlocutors are encouraged to [[Connecting on values - 202307091742\|connect on values]].

As opposed to other traditional methods,[^2] [[projects/How Minds Change/Deep Canvassing - 202307091701\|deep canvassing's]] effect has been shown to be effective and permanent.[^3][^4][^5]

Deep Canvassing is partly effective because it's likely to push your interlocutor into a state of [[projects/How Minds Change/Elaboration - 202307131626\|elaboration]].[^6]
