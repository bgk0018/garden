# Content

>[!Note]  
>This is my own personal term.

The process someone goes through when faced with the process by which they arrived at their own conclusion. This begins with some form of unresolved introspection where people begin to find a more nuanced position if they produce their own counterarguments.[^1][^2]

Just the process of verbal [[Self-talk\|Self-talk]] can trigger this processing.
