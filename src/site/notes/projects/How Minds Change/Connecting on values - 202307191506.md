A way to close the conversation in [[projects/How Minds Change/Deep Canvassing - 202307091701\|Deep Canvassing]] by attempting to provide your position on the issue in the discussion, but in a way that shows it as an alternative to theirs and not condemnation of their position. This should be focused on a shared value or what is important.[^1]
