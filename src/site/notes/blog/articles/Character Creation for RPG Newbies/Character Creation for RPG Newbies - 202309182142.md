# Character Creation for RPG Newbies

Some family members that have never played dungeons and dragons wanted to try and so I came up with this character creation process to help them think through their characters.

# Character Creation

---

>[!FAQ] Archetype  
>In 3-5 words describe the type of character you'd be interested in playing  
>**Examples:**
>- Foul mouthed drunken sailor
>- Golden hearted prison warden
>- Honorable thief
>- Promise bound healer
>- Wizard school drop out

---

>[!FAQ] Adventurer  
> In 1-2 sentences, what motivates your character to adventure?  
> **Examples:**
> - They needed to make money to pay off a debt that their current employment wouldn't be able to met 
> - They crossed the wrong people and are now adventuring hoping they'll be hard to find 
> - They have been commissioned by the institution they're a part of to conduct a field study in their area of expertise 
> - They are adventuring to escape a boring life they left behind

---

>[!FAQ] Inner Connections  
>In one sentence, how are you connected to someone in the group?  
>**Examples:**  
>-We're siblings  
>-We're from the same town  
>-We're from the same schoole

---

>[!FAQ] Outer Connections  
>In one sentence, how are you connected to someone or something outside the group?  
>**Examples:**
>- I'm part of a guild 
>- I know the person who is asking for help 
>- I owe money to a powerful group
