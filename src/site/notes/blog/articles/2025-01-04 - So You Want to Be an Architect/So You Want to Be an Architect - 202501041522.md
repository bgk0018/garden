# So You Want to Be an Architect

Intention

- Find out what does 'architect' mean to us
- What does our current meetup group composition look like
- Share in our experience to help guide aspiring attendees or connect on problems we're trying to solve currently

- Raise your hand if:
	- Currently hold a title with the word 'architect' in it
	- You currently work as a person who is looked to for architectural guidance
	- You have taken on an architect role before (title not important)

We are going to do "Around the World"...

- You will be assigned into small groups
	- You will get markers and sticky notes
	- You will be stationed at a question that the group will discuss for 3 minutes (I will track time and let you know)
	- Place a sticky note or 2 on the question that best represents highlights in your discussion
	- Move to the next question, repeat

You may not get to all the questions

- I think an architect is...
- I think an architect title means...
- I think an architect makes a difference by....
- I think the future of technical architecture will likely involve...
- I think at their worst an architect is...
- I think a challenge architects often face is...

- My company's view of an architect is...
- Advice I would give to an aspiring architect is...
- One misconception about architects is...
- A lesson I learned from a mistake in my architecture career was...

## Conclusion

- Move between each question, "What did we learn?"
