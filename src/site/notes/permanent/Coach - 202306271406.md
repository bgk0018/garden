# Content

Supports a [[permanent/Creator - 202306271318\|Creator]] by asking questions that help clarify the [[permanent/Creator - 202306271318\|Creator]]'s outcomes or helps them to see reality more clearly.[^1]

Unlike a [[permanent/Rescuer - 202305271811\|Rescuer]], a [[permanent/Coach - 202306271406\|Coach]] will never take away a [[permanent/Creator - 202306271318\|Creator]]'s power to choose their own responses and actions.[^2]

There are three things a [[permanent/Coach - 202306271406\|Coach]] helps get clarity on:[^3]

- Clarify the [[permanent/Outcome - 202304261242\|Outcome]] you want to create
- Speak the truth about your current reality ([[permanent/Dynamic Tension - 202304171455\|Dynamic Tension]])
- Get clear about the [[permanent/Baby Step - 202304261244\|Baby Step]]s you're going to take.
