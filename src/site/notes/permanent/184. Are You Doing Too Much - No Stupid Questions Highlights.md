>Unconditional Positive Regard and Perfectionism
>Summary:
>Unconditional positive regard emphasizes the importance of believing in one's self-worth independent of external achievements.
>It is the foundation for a bedrock self-esteem that enables individuals to persevere regardless of the outcome. Perfectionism, on the other hand, does not guarantee success and is not consistently linked to achievement.
>In the age of social media, this belief is challenged, leading individuals to strive excessively for perfection.
>However, understanding that self-worth is not tied to grades or performance can help individuals persist even in the face of imperfection.
