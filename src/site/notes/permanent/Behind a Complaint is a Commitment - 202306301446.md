# Content

Behind a complaint or [[permanent/Problem - 202304261228\|Problem]] is a commitment, or something that the person cares about.[^1]

Understand why the person feels like a [[permanent/Victim - 202305251726\|Victim]] and find what they care about. Then have them focus on the [[permanent/Outcome - 202304261242\|Outcome]] they want.[^2]
