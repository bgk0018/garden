>Understanding the Components of Changing Minds
>Summary:
>Changing minds requires empathy and understanding that human beings are motivated reasoners. It involves beliefs, attitudes, and values which are distinct mental constructs. Beliefs are estimations of truth, attitudes are valenced estimations of positive or negative qualities, and values determine where one puts time, money, and effort. Human minds are constantly changing in these regards through a process called assimilation and accommodation.
