# Content

A [[permanent/Challenger - 202306271330\|Challenger]] sparks learning and growth. This is the opposite of a [[permanent/Persecutor - 202305271800\|Persecutor]]. [[permanent/Challenger - 202306271330\|Challengers]] can be conscious and unconscious to the challenge they pose to the [[permanent/Creator - 202306271318\|Creator]].[^1][^2]

A good challenger knows what their intention is, and knows to challenge when your intention is to encourage learning and growth.[^3][^4]

Unlike a [[permanent/Persecutor - 202305271800\|Persecutor]], a [[permanent/Challenger - 202306271330\|Challenger]] doesn't seek to control, but to be compassionate.[^5]
